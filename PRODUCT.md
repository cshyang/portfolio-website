# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The portfolio serves a mixed audience of hiring leaders, consulting clients, and technical collaborators. They arrive with limited time and need to understand what Shyang can do, see credible proof of shipped work, and find an appropriate way to start a conversation.

## Product Purpose

Present Shyang as a multidisciplinary builder who ships useful systems across data, AI, product, and code. Success means visitors understand that positioning within the first screen, can inspect selected work without decoding a résumé, and leave with enough confidence and warmth to make contact.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, experimental, and resourceful. The experience should feel mature and approachable: opinionated without becoming loud, playful without becoming childish, and technically credible without borrowing developer-terminal aesthetics.

## Anti-references

- Generic polished SaaS landing pages built from glass cards, pastel blobs, and interchangeable feature grids.
- Developer-terminal brutalism that uses darkness, monospace type, or command-line decoration as a shortcut for technical credibility.
- Artsy portfolio layouts that conceal navigation, weaken readability, or make visitors work to find evidence.
- Youthful candy palettes, pervasive faux-vintage noise, and decorative motion without meaning.

## Design Principles

- Lead with capability, then support it with personal context.
- Make proof visible early; projects are evidence, not decoration.
- Create depth through composition, imagery, and behavior rather than excess chrome.
- Let each section move in a way that fits its content while preserving one coherent voice.
- Keep experimental choices legible, useful, and easy to navigate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain readable contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic structure, and full keyboard access. Provide reduced-motion alternatives for every animation. Hover interactions must have equivalent focus and touch behavior, and content must never depend on motion or color alone.
