# Subtle Layered V2 Homepage

## Goal

Replace the homepage's persistent visible grid with a calmer layered composition. Preserve the sharp, experimental identity without making every section compete for attention.

## Composition Rule

Layering is limited to two deliberate moments:

1. The hero portrait sits behind or beside the headline with controlled visual overlap and no text collision.
2. The featured project artifact crosses one content seam by a small amount to create depth.

All remaining content follows normal document flow. Decorative rotation, scattered labels, floating cards, and repeated cell borders are excluded.

## Hero

- Use an open two-part composition instead of bordered grid cells.
- Keep the current headline, supporting copy, CTA, portrait swap, and portrait-derived palette.
- Place the portrait in a restrained blue image field on the right.
- Allow the headline to lead visually; the portrait supports it rather than forming an equal cell.
- Retain one small orange circular mark as the only free-standing decorative layer.
- Preserve readable text and collision-free layout from mobile through desktop.

## Selected Work

- Remove the full-width cell treatment and repeated project borders.
- Introduce the section through whitespace and one hairline divider.
- Present projects as alternating image-and-copy compositions with generous vertical rhythm.
- Let only the first featured artifact overlap its copy surface slightly.
- Keep later projects flat and structurally simpler so the first project remains the focal point.

## Operating Range and Journey

- Keep Operating Range as one full-width deep-indigo color field with animated light and grain.
- Use color contrast and spacing rather than internal boxes to organize its content.
- Present Journey as a linear narrative with minimal rules, not a grid of equal modules.
- Preserve all existing content and navigation anchors.

## Contact

- Keep the form and contact copy in normal flow with a clear two-column desktop layout and single-column mobile layout.
- Avoid surrounding both columns with repeated cell borders.

## Motion

- Retain the portrait swap and Operating Range light-field animation.
- Use one restrained entrance treatment for the hero composition.
- Do not add rotation, parallax, or independent floating motion.
- Preserve reduced-motion behavior.

## Responsive Behavior

- Desktop may use controlled overlap only where specified.
- Tablet reduces overlap before text or imagery becomes compressed.
- Mobile removes overlap and returns every section to a clear single-column reading order.
- The quiet-island desktop navigation and standalone mobile Menu pill remain unchanged.

## Validation

- Verify at 390px, 820px, 1024px, and 1280px.
- Confirm no text-image collisions or horizontal overflow.
- Confirm portrait hover, focus, and tap behavior still works.
- Confirm navigation anchors, contact form, reduced motion, and reduced transparency still work.
- Confirm the original `/` route remains visually and functionally unchanged.
